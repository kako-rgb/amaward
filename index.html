Our Partnership

Amaward Marketing is a marketing company operating in partnership with a Logistics company called SHINE MARITIME. We offer both Below-The-Line (BTL) and Above-The-Line (ATL) marketing solutions. Our partnership ensures market solutions are met, including logistic services achieved seamlessly for all our clients.

With experience across industries such as FMCG, retail, telecommunications, automotive, and Fintech, we bring diverse and actionable insights to every project.

Our BTL Project Modus Operandi

  1. Agent Team Recruitment: Identifying and onboarding the right talent.
  2. Brand & Product Training: Comprehensive training and role-playing as per client guidelines.
  3. Route & Target Setting: Allocating routes/outlets and setting qualitative/quantitative KPIs.
  4. Real-Time Reporting & KPI Capture: Monitoring progress via tools like WhatsApp for timely data.
  5. Target Reward & Motivation: Implementing variable pay structures based on target achievement.
  6. Market Intelligence Gathering: Continuously collecting market feedback for improvement.
